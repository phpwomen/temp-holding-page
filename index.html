Plans for 2014

To further support & mentor women worldwide.

Purple PHPWomen ElePHPants

Another exciting plan for 2014 is for PHPWomen to create its very own Purple ElePHPant.

This is well under way so keep your ears peeled to hear more about it in the upcoming few months!

The Website

Evolving the site to include a lot more community features, making it easier for PHPWomen around the world to connect and collaborate.

Help us and see the progress on github

Education

For 2014 we hope to bring new education initiatives to the community, by standing on the shoulders of the amazing RailsBridge efforts, we hope to bring our very own “PHPBridge” to fruition this year. Starting with introductory material, and moving on from there.

PHPWomen in the US

We are excited to announce that Davey Shafik will be leading the US chapter starting in 2014, meaning PHPWomen will be more active globally.

Working together with the crew in Europe, he will be helping with our education and events initiatives.

Events

We will participate in numerous conferences and user groups this year, both in Europe and the US. As part of this participation we hope to help more women to attend, and speak! We are actively looking for sponsors to aid us in these goals!

If you run a conference or user group and would like to collaborate, please let us know!

Helping Us, Help You

PHPWomen is nothing without it's members and we are always open to new ideas and suggestions.

If you, your company, your conference or user group, would like to help us with any of these intiatives, or have ideas you would like to discuss with us, please email us at supportus@phpwomen.org

Together we can make 2014 a phenomenal year for PHPWomen.
